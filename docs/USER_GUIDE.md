# Criminal Appeal AI - User Guide

## First Time Setup

### Step 1: Sign In
1. Go to the app URL
2. Click **"Sign In with Google"** (top right corner)
3. Click **"Continue with Google"** 
4. Select your Google account to sign in
5. You'll be taken to your Dashboard

---

## Creating Your First Case

### Step 2: Create a New Case
1. On the Dashboard, click **"New Case"** button
2. Fill in the case details:
   - **Case Title**: Name for your case (e.g., "R v Smith Appeal")
   - **Defendant Name**: The person's name
   - **Case Number**: (Optional) Court case reference number
   - **Court**: (Optional) Which court
   - **Summary**: Brief description of the case
3. Click **"Create Case"**
4. Your new case will appear on the Dashboard

---

## Adding Documents

### Step 3: Upload Your Documents
1. Click on your case to open it
2. You'll see the **Documents** tab (default view)
3. Click **"Upload Document"** button
4. Select one or multiple files (PDF, DOCX, TXT, or images)
5. Choose a category:
   - Brief of Evidence
   - Court Transcript
   - Legal Submission
   - Witness Statement
   - Expert Report
   - Police Report
   - Medical Record
   - Correspondence
   - Other
6. Add an optional description
7. Click **"Upload"**

### Step 4: Extract Text from Documents
1. After uploading, click **"Extract Text"** button
   - This reads text from PDF and Word documents
2. For scanned documents or images, click **"OCR Scan"**
   - This uses AI to read text from images
3. Wait for processing to complete
4. Documents with extracted text show a green icon

### Step 5: Search Your Documents
1. Use the search bar in the Documents tab
2. Type any word or phrase
3. Results show which documents contain your search term
4. Matching text is highlighted with context

---

## Building Your Timeline

### Step 6: Generate Timeline Automatically
1. Click the **"Timeline"** tab
2. Click **"AI Generate Timeline"** button
3. Wait 30-60 seconds while AI analyzes your documents
4. The AI will extract all dates and events mentioned
5. Events appear in chronological order

### Step 7: Add Events Manually (Optional)
1. Click **"Add Event"** button
2. Enter:
   - **Title**: What happened
   - **Date**: When it happened
   - **Description**: Details
   - **Event Type**: incident, arrest, court hearing, etc.
3. Click **"Create Event"**

---

## Finding Grounds of Merit

### Step 8: AI Identify Grounds
1. Click the **"Grounds"** tab
2. Click **"AI Identify Grounds"** button
3. Wait 30-60 seconds for AI analysis
4. The AI will find potential appeal grounds from your documents:
   - Procedural errors
   - Fresh evidence
   - Judicial errors
   - Ineffective counsel
   - And more...

### Step 9: Investigate a Ground
1. Find a ground you want to explore
2. Click the **"Investigate"** button on that ground
3. Wait for AI deep analysis
4. You'll get:
   - Relevant law sections (NSW & Federal)
   - Similar cases
   - Supporting evidence from your documents
   - Strategic recommendations

### Step 10: Add Grounds Manually (Optional)
1. Click **"Add Ground"** button
2. Fill in the details
3. Click **"Create Ground"**

---

## Adding Notes & Comments

### Step 11: Create Notes
1. Click the **"Notes"** tab
2. Click **"Add Note"** button
3. Enter:
   - **Title**: Note heading
   - **Content**: Your notes
   - **Category**: General, Legal Opinion, Evidence Note, Strategy, Question, or Action Item
4. Click **"Create Note"**
5. Use the pin icon to keep important notes at the top

---

## Generating Reports

### Step 12: Generate a Report
1. Click the **"Reports"** tab
2. Click **"Generate Report"** button
3. Choose report type:
   - **Quick Summary**: Brief overview of the case
   - **Full Detailed**: Complete analysis with law references
   - **Extensive Log**: Comprehensive documentation
4. Wait 30-60 seconds for AI to generate
5. Click on the report to view it

### Step 13: Export Your Report
1. Open any report
2. Choose export option:
   - **"Export PDF"**: Download as PDF file
   - **"Export Word"**: Download as editable Word document
   - **"Print"**: Print directly
   - **"Barrister View"**: Professional presentation format

---

## Barrister View

### Step 14: Present to Legal Counsel
1. Open any report
2. Click **"Barrister View"** button
3. This shows a clean, professional A4 format with:
   - Case information
   - All Grounds of Merit prominently displayed
   - Law sections and similar cases
   - Supporting evidence
4. Use **"Export PDF"** or **"Print"** from this view

---

## Tips for Best Results

### Document Tips
- Upload clear, readable documents
- Use OCR Scan for scanned/image documents
- Add descriptions to help organize files
- Extract text before using AI features

### AI Features Tips
- The more documents you upload, the better the AI analysis
- AI features may take 30-60 seconds - be patient
- Review AI-generated content and edit as needed
- Use "Investigate" for deeper analysis of specific grounds

### Organization Tips
- Use note categories to organize your thoughts
- Pin important notes to keep them visible
- Add manual timeline events for key dates AI might miss
- Generate reports periodically to track progress

## Need Help?

If something isn't working:
1. Make sure your documents have extracted text (green icon)
2. Wait for AI features to complete (loading spinner)
3. Try refreshing the page
4. Log out and log back in if issues persist
