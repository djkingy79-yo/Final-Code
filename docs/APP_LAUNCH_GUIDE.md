### App Store (iOS) - Main Description

**App Name:** Criminal Appeal AI  
**Subtitle:** Research Your Criminal Appeal

---

**Description:**

**When the justice system fails, knowledge becomes your greatest weapon.**

Criminal Appeal AI is Australia's first AI-powered criminal appeal research tool — built by Deb King, a mother who refused to accept that her loved ones were failed by the system.

**🔍 WHAT THIS APP DOES**

Upload your case documents — transcripts, judgments, police statements, evidence — and let our AI analyse everything to identify potential grounds for appeal that you might have missed.

• **AI-Powered Analysis** — Identifies procedural errors, fresh evidence opportunities, sentencing disparities, and more
• **Similar Case Finder** — Discover successful appeals with links to full AustLII decisions
• **Deadline Tracker** — Never miss a critical filing date with calendar reminders
• **Barrister-Quality Reports** — Generate professional legal briefs you can hand to your lawyer
• **All 8 Jurisdictions** — NSW, VIC, QLD, SA, WA, TAS, NT, ACT + Federal

**📋 THREE REPORT TIERS**

• **Quick Summary (FREE)** — Case overview, grounds preview, deadline reminders
• **Full Report ($29)** — 4-6 similar cases, legislation links, step-by-step appeal guide
• **Extensive Log ($39)** — 8-12 cases, witness analysis, sentencing comparison, complete strategy

**⚠️ IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER**

This app is NOT legal advice. It's a research tool to help you understand your options and organise your case materials. Always consult a qualified lawyer before taking legal action.

## 💬 PRESS RELEASE DRAFT

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Australian Mother Launches AI-Powered App to Help Families Fight Wrongful Convictions**

*Criminal Appeal AI gives everyday Australians access to legal research tools previously only available to expensive lawyers*

GLENMORE PARK, NSW — Deb King knows the pain of watching loved ones be failed by the justice system. After years of researching criminal appeals for family members, she's turned that hard-won knowledge into an app that could help thousands of Australian families.

Criminal Appeal AI uses artificial intelligence to analyse case documents — transcripts, judgments, evidence — and identify potential grounds for appeal that might otherwise go unnoticed.

"I'm not a lawyer," says King. "But I learned that many appeals succeed on technicalities that families never knew existed. This app helps people understand their options."

The app covers all eight Australian jurisdictions and generates professional reports that users can share with legal professionals.

"If this tool helps even one person discover grounds they didn't know existed, my mission is accomplished," King adds.

Criminal Appeal AI is available now on iOS and Android.
